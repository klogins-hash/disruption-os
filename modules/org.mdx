## Overview

**Target:** PE Firms, HR Leaders

**Function:** Analyzing teams/culture for AI adoption

**Output:** The "AI Velocity Score"

## Jobs to Be Done

<Note>
Leveraging HR & Workday consulting background to assess organizational readiness
</Note>

## The AI Velocity Score

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cultural Assessment">
    Is the organization open to AI-driven change?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skills Gap Analysis">
    What capabilities exist vs. what's needed?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Audit">
    Which workflows are AI-ready?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Leadership Alignment">
    Does exec team understand the urgency?
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Outputs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Velocity Score (0-100)">
    Quantified measure of AI adoption readiness
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Skills Roadmap">
    Training and hiring priorities
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Quick Wins">
    Immediate AI opportunities with existing team
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Risk Areas">
    Where resistance or capability gaps exist
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
